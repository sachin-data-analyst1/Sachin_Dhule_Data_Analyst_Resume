## 🎯 About Me

I'm a Data Analyst focused on converting business questions into data-driven answers. My work spans **risk & collections analytics, e-commerce revenue intelligence, and HR workforce insights** — using SQL for data extraction and modeling, Python for cleaning and analysis, and Power BI for storytelling through dashboards.

I care about one thing above all: **does this analysis change a decision?** Every project in this portfolio is built around a real business problem, a measurable KPI, and a clear recommendation — not just a chart.

- 🔍 **Focus areas:** Credit Risk & Collections · E-Commerce Analytics · HR & Workforce Analytics
- 🛠️ **Core stack:** SQL (PostgreSQL/MySQL) · Power BI · Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn) · Excel
- 📊 **What I deliver:** Interactive dashboards, KPI frameworks, and business recommendations backed by data
- 🌱 **Currently sharpening:** Advanced DAX, data modeling, and cloud data warehousing (Azure/Snowflake)
- 🎯 **Career objective:** A Data Analyst role at a global consulting or technology firm where I can apply analytics to solve high-impact business problems
